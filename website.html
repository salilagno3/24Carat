Product Information

The 24-Carat is a stainless-steel, vacuum-insulated chug that keeps drinks cold for up to 24 hours. The LED smart sensor “puck” glows to remind you when it’s time to drink and tracks your water intake by syncing via Bluetooth to the 24-Carat App. The 24-Carat bundle includes both a chug and a straw lid.

How does 24-Carat work?

Throughout each day, 24-Carat STEEL calculates and adjusts a personalized hydration goal based on your body and activity level. The sensor puck tracks how many ounces or milliliters you drink and then records it via the app on your smart phone.

Features


The 24-Carat syncs seamlessly with the 24-Carat PRO STEEL. Once you create an account, the app can access Apple Health for your personal info and step data—which it uses to adjust your daily hydration goal. The app also pushes every sip it records back to Apple Health, allowing you to see all your data in one place. You can also keep track of your consumption on the Apple Watch and easily add amounts of water that you drink from sources other than the 24-Carat STEEL bottle.
