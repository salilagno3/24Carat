About us!

Welcome to the world of 24-Carat!

We are excited to introduce you to our innovative and stylish vacuum-insulated chug that keeps your drinks cold for up to 24 hours. Our product is designed to help you stay hydrated throughout the day by reminding you when it's time to drink and tracking your water intake through our smart sensor "puck" and the 24-Carat App.

Our sensor puck glows to remind you when it's time to drink, and tracks your water intake by syncing via Bluetooth to the 24-Carat App on your smart phone. Our app uses your personal info and step data from Apple Health to calculate and adjust a personalized hydration goal based on your body and activity level. With seamless integration with Apple Health, you can easily track your consumption and see all your data in one place.

The 24-Carat STEEL bundle includes both a chug and a straw lid, giving you the flexibility to drink your water the way you like it. Whether you're on the go or working out, 24-Carat is the perfect companion to keep you hydrated and healthy. Join the 24-Carat community today and take the first step towards a healthier and more hydrated you!
